Give visitors the means to tell their own stories.

Hybrid museum experiences allow visitors to co-create their own stories by blending the physical museum with the digital medium. Adopt or adapt our tools, or use them to create your own hybrid museum experience.

Backed by the latest research on experience design, we provide a framework that aims to support museums in developing hybrid experiences based on gifting and play.

Give visitors the means to share experiences through play.

We have developed and tested tools and experiences that use play as a means of engaging visitors with your collection.

Give visitors the means to share and gift objects to one another.

Visitors can add their own digital content to existing museum objects and ‘gift’ these objects to one another. We call these hybrid gifting experiences.

Adopt our playtested gifting app, or use our platform to design or create your own gifting experience.

GIFT is an EU-funded Horizon 2020 Research Project.

Bringing together artists, designers, museum professionals and computer scientists, the project explores hybrid forms of museum experiences to help museums create personal encounters with cultural heritage - both in physical and digital forms.
