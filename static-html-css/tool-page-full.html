Let your visitors give a personalised museum experience to someone they care about.

The Gift Exchange App lets visitors share museum experiences in a more personal and engaged manner than most social media allow. Visitors assemble objects or places in the museum as an ‘experience’ to gift to someone they care about. The gift is digitally wrapped and sent to the recipient, who can unwrap and experience the gift then and there, or visit the museum to get the full experience.

What is the experience like?

Some people created playful treasure hunts with a game-like mission to find all the objects, while others spent a great deal of time and care choosing a single object. See how the app has been used by:

  • Brighton Museum & Art Gallery
  • The University of Nottingham Museum of Archaeology

How can we use it?

The Gift Exchange app can be used in your museum without any particular technical resources. All you need to do is register with us and add some placeholder content.

Why not social media?

The Gift Exchange app facilitates private sharing between single individuals. Therefore it requires less moderation and control than ordinary social media. The design of the Gift Exchange App is based on well-backed research on:

  • Gifting
  • Intimate Experiences

Visitors who use the Gift Exchange app tend to explore the museum exhibitions carefully, searching for objects that have personal meaning for them and the recipient of the gift. Here’s an overview of how it works:

... It was a chance to experiment and understand a more detailed model of social sharing, and one that’s beyond the more performative style of most social media activity. How do people identify and choose to share the value they perceive in the displayed collections?
